    Cooking the meat and sauce:

  1. In a skillet, add the meat and 3/4 cup of chopped onions with 2 Tbsp olive oil.
  2. Season meat with 1 Tbsp parsley, 1/2 tsp basil, 1/2 tsp pepper,
    1/2 tsp oregano, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp sugar, and 2 tsp salt.
  3. Cook over medium-high heat until meat is browned. Drain some of the fat grease.
  4. In the large sauce, add in 2 cups of water, 16 oz of crushed tomatoes, and 6 oz of tomato paste.
  5. While sauce cooks for 45 minutes, boil lasagna sheets until "al dente" (not overly cooked)
    and drain the water.

    Assembly:

  1. Add a light layer of sauce to the bottom of a baking pan.
  2. Layer lasagna sheet on the bottom and spread cottage cheese, mozzarella, and sauce evenly.
  3. Repeat ending with your sauce ontop. Cover with cheese and sprinkle parmesan generously on top.
  4. Either freeze or refridgerate for up to 2 days or oven bake immediately at 350-degrees for 45-60 minutes.
